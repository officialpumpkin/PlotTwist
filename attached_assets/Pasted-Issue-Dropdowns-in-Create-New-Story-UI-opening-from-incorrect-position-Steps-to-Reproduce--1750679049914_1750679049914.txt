Issue: Dropdowns in "Create New Story" UI opening from incorrect position.

Steps to Reproduce:

    Open the "Create New Story" window.

    Click on any of the following dropdowns:

        Genre

        Word Limit per Turn

        Character Limit per Turn

        Max Story Segments

Expected Behavior:
Each dropdown should open from the location of its corresponding button.

Actual Behavior:
All four dropdowns open from the top-left corner of the screen, rather than directly below their buttons.

Impact:
This misalignment makes the interface harder to use and visually inconsistent.
